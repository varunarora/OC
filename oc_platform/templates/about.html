OpenCurriculum is a place to create, access and share K-12 teaching materials

Our mission is to bring openness and innovation to K-12 education around the world

We believe that every child in the world deserves access to a high-quality early education. Such an education empowers individuals to grow and get access to opportunities better than ever before, and thus drives economic development.

Our philosophy

A region's economic development is hugely impacted by education of its citizens. While higher education is a major enabler in helping individuals serve in industries, it is really early education, primarily K-12 education, that defines the motivations and intentions of individuals. These are critical in shaping individuals and societies they are a part of.

We believe that curriculum, or what people learn during their years of schooling, alongside mentorship, educational environment, etc. plays a vital role in building people's wisdom. Curricula ought to be constantly critically examined, as it defines what we think about and what we do. We have for long ignored it; it needs a renaissance and we need to challenge the status quo.

Vision

Our vision is to ensure access to high-quality K-12 education opportunities for every single child in the world. To us, high-quality is as important as universal access is. But it goes further than meeting standards of examination and instruction quality. In our definition of "high-quality", community, innovation and freedom are an integral part.

Proudly non-profit

Making profit is great - it allows organizations to create additional incentive for investors to engage in creating value. But we are proudly, consciously and loudly a non-profit organization.

We believe that profit may come in the way of our mission and the value we create. Not only are we fully committed to re-investing our revenues into the organization and only the organization itself, we are deeply connected to our values and long-term goals. We are driven to giving back in the best possible way we can, while running a fast-growing highly-competitive venture.

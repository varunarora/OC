We're always looking for the finest talent in engineering, design and content that is deeply passionate about addressing core K-12 education challenges around the world. For internships and other roles listed below, contact careers [at] theopencurriculum [dot] org.

Founding Software Engineer

OpenCurriculum is attempting to be a Github for textbooks that allows communities to collaboratively create and share K-12 curricula material.

We are trying to solve the problems of lack of access to free textbooks, lack of innovation, and lack of technology readiness of education systems for more than 100 million learners. Our mission is to make K-12 education open and innovative around the world. Thus, we pride ourselves in trying to become the Mozilla of education.

In a Founding Software Engineer, we seek a star hacker who loves scalability, security and testing and doesn't fear working all across the stack. Our platform has to perform complex operations of managing contribution concurrency, Git-like revision management, and user profile+feed management. In addition, he/she will be required to optimize user experiences for web and tablets.

Requirements:
  • BSc., MSc. or PhD. in CS, ECE or related field from a top-tier university
  • Proficiency in programming, preferrably in Python and/or JavaScript
  • Working familiarity with HTML & CSS. SASS experience preferred
  • Experience with SQL databases and web server configuration and management is a plus
  • Good communication skills and healthy disregard for the impossible
  • Willingness to learn rapidly on the fly
